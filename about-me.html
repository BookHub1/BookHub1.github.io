What is BookHub?

BookHub is your place to buy, sell, and trade books and cool items. Sellers can list items with prices and descriptions. Buyers can save favorites and build a cart.

Authentication uses real Google Sign-In so your saved items and listings are tied to your Google account — no passwords needed.

Payments are handled by a Stripe-inspired checkout UI with support for multiple saved cards. A real Stripe integration can be wired in with a backend key.

Features

🔐

Real Google Sign-In

Google One Tap — no passwords, no fake mocks. Your account is your Google account.

🛒

Cart & Checkout

Add items, adjust quantities, and checkout with a Stripe-like payment UI.

💳

Multiple Saved Cards

Save up to 5 payment cards. Switch between them at checkout. Securely stored in your browser.

📋

Sell Anything

List a book or item in seconds with the live-preview listing form.

🌙

Dark / Light Mode

Full dark mode support. Your preference is remembered across sessions.
